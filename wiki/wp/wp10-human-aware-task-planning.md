# WP10: Human-Aware Task Planning

## Goal
Plan robot tasks that respect human presence, intentions, and safety in the workspace.

## Methods
| Sub-task | Methods |
|------ ----|-- ----- --|
| **WP10.1 Task Planning** | Classical planners (PDDL), learning-based planners |
| **WP10.2 Human-Aware Planning** | Constraint satisfaction, intent-aware planning |

## Integration
Task plans feed into WP11 (action recommendation) and WP7 (motion planning).

## Validation
Compare on task completion rate, plan quality, and human satisfaction.
