# WP9: Robot Control and Motion Generation

## Goal
Generate low-level control commands for the robot to execute planned motions safely.

## Methods
| Sub-task | Methods |
|------ ----|-- ----- --|
| **WP9.1 Motion Generation** | Trajectory optimization, reinforcement learning |
| **WP9.2 Safety Control** | Control barrier functions, MPC |

## Integration
Control commands feed into the robot's actuators.

## Validation
Compare on tracking accuracy, safety margins, and smoothness of motion.
