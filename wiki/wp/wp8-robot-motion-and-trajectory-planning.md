# WP8: Robot Motion and Trajectory Planning

## Goal
Generate safe, efficient, and socially-compliant motion trajectories for the robot.

## Methods
| Sub-task | Methods |
|------ ----|-- ----- --|
| **WP8.1 Trajectory Generation** | RRT*, APF, MPC-based planners |
| **WP8.2 Social Compliance** | Social force models, comfort zones |

## Integration
Generated trajectories feed into WP11 (action recommendation).

## Validation
Compare on path optimality, collision avoidance, social compliance, and computation time.
