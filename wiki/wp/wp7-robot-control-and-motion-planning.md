# WP7: Robot Control and Motion Planning

## Goal
Plan robot motion paths that are safe, efficient, and socially aware in the workspace.

## Methods
| Sub-task | Methods |
|------ ----|-- ----- --|
| **WP7.1 Collision Avoidance** | DWA, APF, MPC-based planners |
| **WP7.2 Trajectory Optimization** | Optimal control, reinforcement learning |

## Integration
Motion plans feed into WP11 (action recommendation).

## Validation
Compare on collision rate, path optimality, computation time, and comfort (jerk/smoothness).
