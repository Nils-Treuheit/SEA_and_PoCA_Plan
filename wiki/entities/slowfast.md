# SlowFast Networks

## Summary

Two-pathway video action recognition architecture — slow pathway for semantic content, fast pathway for motion. Proposed for WP2.2 action recognition.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP2.2 — Action Recognition
- **Strengths**: Strong on video benchmarks (Kinetics), captures both appearance and motion
- **Limitations**: Heavy, not real-time by default
