# Video Swin Transformer

## Summary

Swin Transformer adapted for video action recognition with shifted window self-attention. Proposed for WP2.2.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP2.2 — Action Recognition
- **Strengths**: Hierarchical features, strong on Kinetics benchmark
- **Limitations**: Computationally heavy, requires large GPU memory
