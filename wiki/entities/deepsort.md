# DeepSORT

## Summary

Extension of SORT with deep appearance embeddings for re-identification. Proposed for WP4.2 multi-object tracking.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP4.2 — Continuous Multi-Object Tracking
- **Strengths**: Appearance-based re-ID, handles short occlusions well
- **Limitations**: Heavy appearance feature computation, track confusion in crowded scenes
