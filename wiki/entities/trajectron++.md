# Trajectron++

## Summary

Probabilistic, multimodal trajectory prediction framework with social relationship modeling. Proposed for WP2.1.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP2.1 — Human Trajectory Prediction
- **Strengths**: Probabilistic (uncertainty estimates), multimodal, graph-based social relationships
- **Limitations**: More complex setup than Social-LSTM
