# ST-GCN (Spatio-Temporal Graph Convolutional Network)

## Summary

Skeleton-based action/gesture recognition using GCNs over joint connections. Proposed for WP1.3 gesture recognition.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP1.3 — Gesture Recognition
- **Strengths**: Model joint topology naturally, efficient, strong on skeleton benchmarks
- **Limitations**: Requires good skeleton input, struggles with dense scenes
