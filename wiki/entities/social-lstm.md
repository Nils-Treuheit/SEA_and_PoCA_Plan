# Social-LSTM

## Summary

LSTM-based trajectory prediction that incorporates social interactions between agents via social pooling. Proposed for WP2.1.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP2.1 — Human Trajectory Prediction
- **Strengths**: Captures social interactions, well-established in the field
- **Limitations**: Limited to short-term predictions, struggles with long-horizon
