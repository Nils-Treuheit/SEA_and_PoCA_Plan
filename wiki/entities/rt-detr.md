# RT-DETR (Real-Time Detection Transformer)

## Summary

Transformer-based real-time object detector that replaces the backbone + head of YOLO with a transformer architecture. Proposed for WP1.1 human detection.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP1.1 — Human Detection Module
- **Strengths**: Transformer architecture, better long-tail detection than YOLO
- **Limitations**: May be heavier than YOLO for edge deployment
