# MediaPipe Pose

## Summary

Google's lightweight real-time pose estimation. Proposed for WP1.2. Runs well on mobile/edge devices.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP1.2 — Human Pose Estimation
- **Strengths**: Extremely lightweight, real-time on mobile, easy to deploy
- **Limitations**: Less accurate than heavier models, single-person focus
