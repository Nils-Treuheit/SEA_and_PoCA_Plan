# TrackR-CNN

## Summary

End-to-end multi-object tracker that couples segmentation and tracking in a unified architecture. Proposed for WP4.2.

## Key Points

- **Use in plan**: WP4.2 — Continuous Multi-Object Tracking
- **Strengths**: Unified detection + tracking, no separate re-ID module needed
- **Limitations**: Complex training, may not generalize well to new domains
